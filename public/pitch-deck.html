DREAMFUND
Problem Statement

The World Cup generates billions.
Almost none goes back.

💰 The Money Problem

FIFA generated $7.5B from the 2022 World Cup. Grassroots football programs in qualifying nations remain chronically underfunded.

🔍 The Transparency Problem

Traditional charity donations are opaque. Donors never know if their money actually reached the project — and there's no emotional connection to the outcome.

😔 The Engagement Problem

Fans want to give back but have no direct, transparent way to fund projects in the countries they're cheering for. Watching feels passive.
